U.S. Federal Courts

Redesigned for the People

Our purpose was simple - to connect people to the U.S. Federal Courts. We believe our communities benefit when the voice of people can be heard and when they can take an active role in the Judicial Branch. However, that is not an easy task in the digital era. So did we do? Investigated any possible barriers and find some creative solutions.


How did we achieve our goal?

The Federal Courts is a complex institutions. So immediately, we decided to focus on one task - increasing the usage of the eJuror Program. We believe that following users through this one task would gives us insights into larger programs of the site, like navigation issues. Through changes to the navigation, terminology, and user flow, we were able to guide more users into the eJuror Program.
